About

Crime Tattoo Studio was founded by Harish, a certified professional tattoo artist with over 7+ years of experience in the tattoo industry. Since 2019, he has been dedicated to creating unique, high-quality tattoos that reflect the personality and story of every client.


With a strong focus on creativity, precision, hygiene, and customer satisfaction, Crime Tattoo Studio has earned the trust of tattoo enthusiasts across Salem and beyond. From custom designs and realistic portraits to traditional and modern tattoo styles, every piece is crafted with passion and attention to detail.


Over the years, we have proudly served 7,000+ happy customers, helping thousands of clients turn their ideas into meaningful artwork. Whether it's your first tattoo or your next masterpiece, we are committed to providing a safe, professional, and memorable experience.

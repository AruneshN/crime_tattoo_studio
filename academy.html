The Best Tattoo Training in Salem

Crime Tattoo Academy is the training arm of Crime Tattoo Studio — open to artists, designers, and anyone ready to turn tattooing into a career. We bring 8+ years of studio experience into the classroom, so every lesson is grounded in what actually works on real skin, with real clients.


Students start with the fundamentals — sketching, shading, and machine setup — before practising on synthetic skin and eventually working with real clients under direct supervision. Beyond technique, we cover hygiene standards, client handling, and the day-to-day of running a studio, so graduates leave ready for the industry, not just the needle.
